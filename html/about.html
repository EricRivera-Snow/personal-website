I have many personal interests, which include the following:

Snowboarding

  • I have been snowbarding since 2008 and have worked to improve my riding every year.
  • I am an instructor at a mountain in New Hampshire, where I teach beginner and intermediate riders the foundational skills required to get down the mountain in a fun and effective way.
  • I recently picked up splitboarding, which is where I use a snowboard that can transition to skis to enable me to climb mountains. This requires specific avalance and outdoor navigation knowledge.

Skateboarding

  • I've recently picked up skateboarding, both short and long boards.
  • When in college, I used to downhill longboard, with racing leathers and a full-face helment.
  • Now I primarly shortboard, focusing on trick progressions, especially ones that can supplement my Skateboarding.

Concerts

  • I've seen so many shows and gone to a few big music festivals, like When We Were Young and Adjacent Festival. I have seen bands like Blink-182, My Chemical Romance, A Day to Remember, Pierce the Veil, the Used, Taking Back Sunday, and many, many more.
